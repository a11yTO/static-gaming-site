The Event

#a11yTO Gaming offers a mixture of presentations, demos, and lightning talks to provide a fun and informative day of learning.

Whether you're a game developer, a gamer, or both, #a11yTO Gaming has you covered. Join us Saturday, October 23rd for a fun day of accessible gaming.

Please note: #a11yTO Talks are shared from Toronto, Canada (UTC-4 hours)
